About Me

Hey, I'm Brett, a Web Developer based in Toronto. I am all about coding slick, interactive web applications. I always try and keep best practices in mind with my work and have a particular affinity for clean, modular code and styling difficult form elements. I am always working to make my projects as accessible and responsive as possible. Currently, I spend most of my coding time in React, Javascript and Sass.

Prior to learning to code, I spent five years in the media industry doing customer care and technical support for web and mobile apps, so I know the importance of user experience. That background motivated me to look at code from the other side, choosing to pursue my career as a developer. When I'm not coding, I can usually be found playing strategy games and browsing reddit for way too long on my home PC. I also love going out for drinks and food at new places with friends and colleagues.
